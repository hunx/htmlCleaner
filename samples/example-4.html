Apache Corporation is a leading independent Oil and Gas exploration and development company with core operations in the United States, Canada, Egypt, the North Sea (UK) and Western Australia. The UK operation, based in Aberdeen, currently has the following vacancies:
 
STRUCTURAL TECHNICAL AUTHORITY
 
The Structural Technical Authority provides specialist technical support for Structural Engineering discipline across all Apache North Sea assets and ensures technical decisions are in line with best practice, legislation and meet corporate Standards and Guidelines.
 
The job holder is responsible for pro-actively contributing to delivery of an effective Integrity Management Service by provision of all structural related activities and strategies.
 
 In addition the Structural Technical Authority provides support to the Integrity Department, where required, with respect to planning, budgeting and reporting and to the Asset, Operations and Maintenance teams, Integrity & Inspection Engineers and the Asset Technical Assistants plus will interface with platform Offshore Inspection Engineers and the Asset Structural Vendors.
 
The ideal candidate is degree qualified in mechanical or related discipline and has attained Chartered Engineer status in addition to having sound kknowledge of relevant legislation), demonstrating a commitment to Step Change in Safety, is an effective communicator and has a demonstrable track record in sub-contractor management.
